## Overview

Shohoj Hishab is a unit and land measurement converter app. We respect your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting any personal information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data.**

- No account or sign-in is required or offered.
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising libraries are included in the app.
- No personal information (name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or files) is accessed or transmitted.
- All conversions are calculated on your device.

## Internet Use

The Currency tab fetches publicly available exchange rates from a third-party service (open.er-api.com). This request does not include any personal information about you. If you are offline, the app uses previously saved rates stored only on your device. No other feature of the app requires an internet connection.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Any app preferences (such as language or theme selection) are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted anywhere. Uninstalling the app removes them.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads or external links directed at children.

## Third-Party Services

The only third-party service contacted is the exchange-rate API described above. We encourage you to review their policies at https://www.exchangerate-api.com. No other third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks are used.

## Disclaimer on Land Measurements

Land unit values follow common regional standards and may differ locally. Always verify measurements with official land records before any transaction. The app is provided for convenience and informational purposes only.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated at this same URL, and the effective date above will be revised.
